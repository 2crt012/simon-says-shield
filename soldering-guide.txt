Soldering Guide:
1. LEDs
  - They look half-octagons with legs of different lenghts.
  - Anode/Cathode is specified by the length of the legs, instert the LED accordingy.
  - The color is specified by the "R/G/B/Y" letters in the corner of the bulb.
  - R is for red.
  - G is for green.
  - B is for blue.
  - Y is for yellow.
2. Buttons
  - They look like a circle in a square, with the contact points at the sides.
  - The orientation doesn't matter.
3. Resistors
 - They look like squares with contact points at the end.
 - Their resistance is specified with the number inside in Ohms.
4. Headers
  - They are just contact points.
